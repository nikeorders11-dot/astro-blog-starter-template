Who does the work

Backgrounds that meet in the middle.

Patent work sits at the intersection of technical understanding and legal precision — our team is built around both. Members bring backgrounds spanning engineering and the sciences alongside intellectual property law, which means the person reading your invention actually understands how it works before they write a word about it.

How we work

What you can expect from an engagement.

Plain-language communication

Most of our clients are inventors and founders, not lawyers. We explain search results, drafts, and office actions in terms that make sense without a legal background — and we're always available to walk through anything that isn't clear.

A narrow search gives false confidence. We search across multiple jurisdictions and both patent and non-patent literature before telling you where you stand.

120

Honest advice, even when it's not what you want to hear

Built for founders, not enterprise budgets
